I presented this talk at the Early Career and Student Statistician’s Conference in 2021. I found that while I learned a lot at university about statistics and data, I didn’t learn a great deal about what it’s like practising statistics in an applied setting. So I made this talk, where I talk about all the things I learned in the first few years of working after graduating from university.

If you have any thoughts about the video or wanted to chat about your experience transitioning out of the student life, let me know on Twitter!
